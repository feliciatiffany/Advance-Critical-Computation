The second word that came to my mind was "Design." I wanted to explore a universal word and, as a design student, I challenged myself to work with "Design" as a concept. I immediately associated it with communication design, particularly motion graphics. Having previously taken a typography class while minoring in communication design, I learned how elements like placement, color, font choice, and word positioning can create a strong impact. Building on that foundation, I aim to experiment with various motion graphics and typography effects commonly seen in video intros, commercials, websites, and other digital media.
